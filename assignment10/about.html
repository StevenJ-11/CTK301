Who Am I?

Hello there, friend... My name is Steven. I'm a composer, producer, and artist. I made this website to show YOU all of the neat little things that I make. My dream, my goal, my One Piece is to work in video games, movies, and/or animation as a composer and sound designer. It might not be super obvious when listening, but my biggest inspirations are Koji Kondo, Nobuo Uematsu, and John Williams. If you haven't noticed, I've got a few nifty links at the bottom of this here page that can take you to my pages on other sites. Feel free to check them out or send me a message that I will DEFINITELY respond to...
